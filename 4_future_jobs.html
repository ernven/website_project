Future jobs in IT

Below you can see some job postings in the ICT field in Finland (source: duunitori.fi). At the time of writing this, there is really so many of them and they are all very varied. In the field of Data they usually require good higher education plus experience in the field. For developers the choices are more varied, and many places don't require extensive experience at all, promising to "learn on the job".

The skill set required is also very different depending on the position. Python is a must for Data analysts. PHP, JS and its frameworks are very common requirements for web developers. For Apps and programs, it's usually C and its variants, and Java for Android.
